# Dr. Covid 

> Dr. Covid is an application that is aimed at aiding in the reopeningg of business by
safeguarding the people that get to enter the business, so that it doesnt become a 
point of spread of the covid disease. It is based on the bluetrace especification for 
data anonimity as well as tracking phone contact through low energy bluetooth.
